Jiaming CHEN


I am a post-doctoral fellow at Goethe University Frankfurt and part of SFB 326 - GAUS. My mentors are Prof. Dr. A. Küronya and Prof. Dr. J. Stix.
Previously, I was a postdoc at IECL working with Prof. D. Brotbek; before it, I was a research assistant at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin.
I obtained my Ph.D. from Université de Paris in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. B. Klingler.
